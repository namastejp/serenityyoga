Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2025 | Effective Date: April 1, 2025

Serenity Yoga Co., Ltd. ("we," "our," or "us"), located at 2-14-3 Shibuya, Shibuya-ku, Tokyo, Japan 〒150-0002, is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our website visitors. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at www.serenityyoga.jp.

By using our website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use our website:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time and duration of visits, referring website, browser type and version, operating system, and IP address.
  • Device Information: Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet), screen resolution, and language settings.
  • Cookies and Tracking Data: Small data files stored on your device to remember preferences and analyze site usage.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us by email or phone, we may record your name, email address, and the content of your message.

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as financial data, health records, or government identification numbers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website and content quality
  • To analyze website traffic patterns and user behavior to enhance user experience
  • To respond to your questions, comments, and support requests
  • To monitor and ensure the security and proper functioning of our website
  • To comply with applicable laws and legal obligations in Japan
  • To prevent fraudulent or unauthorized access to our website

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files placed on your device by your browser when you visit our site.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your language and display preferences.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain features of the website. For more information on managing cookies, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to help us operate our website. These include:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data. Google's privacy policy applies to data collected through this service. You can opt out using Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Web Hosting Providers: Our website is hosted on servers that may log basic access data.

These third-party services have their own independent privacy policies. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. Analytics data is typically retained for 26 months. Contact information is retained for up to 2 years from the date of last contact.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable and appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These include SSL encryption (HTTPS), restricted access to personal data, and regular security reviews.

However, please note that no internet transmission or electronic storage method is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights Under Japanese Law

Under Japan's Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI) and applicable regulations, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: You may request disclosure of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: You may request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Deletion: You may request deletion of your personal information in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Restrict Use: You may request that we stop using or providing your personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@serenityyoga.jp or by post at our address below. We will respond within a reasonable time, generally within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete such information.

9. International Users

Our website is primarily operated in Japan. If you access our website from outside Japan, please be aware that your information may be transferred to and processed in Japan, where data protection laws may differ from your country.

10. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any website you visit.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
